1.1.9 Project: Create an Acai bowl

In this game, players will be able to make their desired Acai bowl by choosing certain fruits, toppings, and sauces to go on top. The turtle will then work to deliver your dream bowl onto your screen! There are a total of 4 fruits, 3 toppings, and 2 sauces, so there are countless possibilities.

1.2.5 Project: Minecraft Fruit Catcher

This is a fruit-catching game in which you are supposed to catch the apples and avoid the poisonous fruit, in a Minecraft theme. This game features a leaderboard with the top 3 scores, the score, the 3 lives that you have each attempt, and progressive difficulty that makes the game hard as your score goes up. The player uses arrow keys to control the basket on the bottom and catch as many fruit as possible!

1.3.1 Project: Easter Egg Hunt

In this game, players will control the cartoon bunny to collect all of the eggs. Once all 10 eggs are collected, the game will ask for your name, and an Easter card pops up with a message to brighten your day!

Scratch Project: Pencil catcher

You aim to collect as many pencils as you can while avoiding trying to dodge the big erasers. Try to get the high score!
